Overview

Purpose

To get a lot of people to be able to find my site when they are looking for things to do in Idaho. to drive rafting trip sales, and provide necessary documentation such as waivers, bookings, etc.

Audience

Target Audience is People ages 25-45 who are going on vacation, who are sporty, athletic, and staying for an extended visit. What do they want? Fun, safe experiences. Building memories with family What do they expect to find? Availability, information about what to expect on a trip, directions, costs. what are their needs that aren't being met? Adventure, fun, excitement, stress relief. How will my audience usually access my site. Mobile device primarily, but needs to be available in other formats
